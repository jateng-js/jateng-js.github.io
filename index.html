Why Jateng JS

For developers who want a local JavaScript room.

Indonesia already has strong city-based JavaScript communities like SurabayaJS and JogjaJS. Jateng JS brings that same spirit to Central Java: practical talks, friendly mentoring, and a place where students, professionals, founders, and hobbyists can grow together.

What happens here

A community built around doing the work.

Meetups

Regular talks, demos, and hallway conversations for frontend, backend, tooling, and product engineering.

Learning circles

Small groups for beginners and experienced developers to study JavaScript, TypeScript, Node.js, Vue, React, Astro, and more.

Project nights

Open sessions to ship community projects, help local organizations, and practice working in public.
